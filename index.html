What we do:

READ Centers provide education and economic opportunities to more than 2 million rural villagers. We establish community library and resource centers (READ Centers) in rural villages in South Asia, where a majority of women are illiterate, and most families live below the poverty line. All of our Centers are owned and operated by the local community, and offer training programs to people of all ages and backgrounds.
